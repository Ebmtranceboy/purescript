PureScript Pop

By David Lettier

To operate the pop machine, drag a coin to the coin slot. After the pop drops, click it to take it. Keep inserting coins and taking pops until the machine is empty. You can refresh the page to reload the machine.

Built with PureScript using functional reactive programming behaviors and events. For more info, head over to Your Easy Guide to Functional Reactive Programming (FRP) .
